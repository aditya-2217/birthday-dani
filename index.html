CHAPTER THREE

Sixteen Little Things

Some of the little things that make me smile whenever I think of you.

You have one of those smiles that's impossible not to smile back at.

It somehow makes everything around it feel a little lighter.

I'll never miss a chance to look for butterflies anymore.

You'll always know why.

Some of my favorite memories are the ones where you were just sitting behind me while we rode around with nowhere important to be.

We never really needed a plan.

Somehow getting lost together always felt like the plan.

Sharing my favorite ice cream with you at your place became one of those little memories that still makes me smile.

Funny how one conversation on a rooftop changed everything.

I still think about that night.

Having you at my home is one of those memories that quietly became really important to me.

Thank you for letting me show you my city.

I don't think Bengaluru has ever been more fun to explore.

I still can't believe you came to watch the team I hated...

Just because it was the team you loved.

I think I spent more time watching you than the match.

I'm still impressed we managed to sneak through Grand Mercure like we actually belonged there.

I still laugh every time I think about it.

I don't think I'll ever hear "Mysuru" without thinking about you.

Some trips stay with you because of the place.

That one stayed because of the company.

I can't hear a Mt. Joy song anymore without you immediately popping into my head.

It's funny how something so small can carry such a big memory.

Every time I think about that little elephant, I think about you.

I don't think either of us planned on talking every single day.

I'm really glad we do.

Of all our memories...

That's still the one I wish had ended differently.

Out of all the places in the world...

I'm really glad one of them was India.

Because that's where I met you.
